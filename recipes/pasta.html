Pasta Recipe

Description

This recipe will teach you how to achieve the perfect pasta.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Salt
  • Sauce
  • Butter
  • Water

Steps

  1. Boil the water.
  2. Add salt.
  3. Add the pasta and wait for 18 minutes.
  4. Take the pasta out of the water and filter it.
  5. Add butter and tomato sauce to your pot.
  6. Add your pasta back and mix.
  7. There you are, bon appetite!
