Boiled Egg Recipe

Description

This recipe will teach you how to achieve the perfect boiled egg.

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Boil the water.
  2. Add salt.
  3. Add the egg and wait for 20 minutes.
  4. Take the egg out and peel it.
  5. Slice the egg and add oil and pepper.
  6. There you are, bon appetite!
