Scrambled Egg Recipe

Description

This recipe will teach you how to achieve the perfect scrambled egg.

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Oil
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs in a bowl.
  2. Scramble them enough that their color will be one.
  3. Add oil to your pan and put your scrambled eggs in.
  4. Add cheese to the pan.
  5. Wait till the cheese melts and eggs becomes hard.
  6. There you are, bon appetite!
